Who We Are

Navoyug - friendship and beyond!!

Welcome to the official website of the alumni association of JNV Nalbari batch of 1998-2005. Our association was created with the aim of fostering a sense of community among our alumni and giving back to our institution and society.

Our association provides a platform for navodayan alumni to expand their professional network, find job opportunities, and receive career counseling. We also provide support to juniors and build partnerships with counterparts from other schools to tackle problems and ignite expert activism.

Our association is committed to promoting the goodness of society and making a positive impact on the world. We believe that by working together, we can achieve great things.
